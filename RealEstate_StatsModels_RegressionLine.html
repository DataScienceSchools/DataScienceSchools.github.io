Finding yhat (Multiple Regression Equation)¶

- Multiple Regression Equation: (Size & View vs. Price)

    - yhat = 7.748e+04 + 218.7521 * df_copy['size'] + 5.756e+04 * df_copy['view']

- NotSeaView -> df_copy['view'] = 0 

- SeaView -> df_copy['view'] = 1
